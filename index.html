Methodology & caveats

Scoring: score_for_user_goal() from hbar_backtest_v2.py. Rewards short-duration profitable deals. Penalizes long holds, low win rates, high drawdowns, and small sample sizes.

Hard gates: deals ≥ 8, win rate ≥ 30%, max DD ≤ 40%, avg duration ≤ 36h.

Pareto dominance: (score ↑, avg_dur ↓, max_dd ↓). A new config is dominated if a prior one is at least as good on all three and strictly better on at least one.

Promotion: at end of each full pass, the loop compares research-best against the live bots.json baseline. Only if score improves by > 0.05 is the new config written to bots.json. The daemon must be restarted manually to pick up new params.

What this report does NOT do: live trading, walk-forward validation across multiple time windows, regime tagging, multi-pair correlation analysis, or fee-aware optimization beyond the per-side 0.26% assumption. The current data is a static snapshot — re-runs of the loop will refresh this report.
